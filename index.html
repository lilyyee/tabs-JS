about lily

Registered Nurse turned Web Developer

history

I spent years reviewing thousands of medical cases as a registered nurse for insurance companies. I was at the top of my game earning a six-figure salary working from home. However, I was stressed out and unhappy.

In 2019, I decided to take a career break for a self-care sabbatical. I started a wellness coaching business and by accident, I quickly became so fascinated with web development after I built my first website. After that, I decided to leave my registered nursing career behind.

background

  • Health & Life Coach Certification from Health Coach Institute

goals

I'm a web developer who wants to build powerful sites and applications that are simple, easy to understand, and functional. I aim to use my developer skills to promote wellness, education, and hopefully, make life simpler.
